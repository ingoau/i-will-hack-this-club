// 01 — the joke

Wait, what?

Hack Club is a nonprofit network of student-run coding clubs and a global Slack community of teenage hackers. The whole point of Hack Club is that members already are hackers.

So when a Hack Clubber says "i wIlL hAcK tHiS cLuB", they mean they're going to hack their own hacking club. It's recursive. It's beautiful. It makes no sense and perfect sense at the same time.

This is that website. A monument to the most circular threat in computing history. We are Hack Club. We will hack Hack Club.
