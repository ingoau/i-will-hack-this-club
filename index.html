// 01 — about

What is this club?

We are a student-run hacking club dedicated to the art and science of offensive and defensive security. Whether you're a complete beginner who just discovered Kali Linux or a seasoned CTF veteran, there's a place for you here.

We meet weekly to break things, fix things, and argue about the right way to spell "pwned". Our activities span Capture The Flag competitions, live vulnerability research, tool-building workshops, and the occasional ethically-questionable pizza purchase.

Everything we do is ethical and legal — we only hack systems we own or have explicit permission to test. (Seriously. Don't be that person.)

// 02 — what we hack

Our Domains

Security is a broad field. We cover a little bit of everything.

🌐

Web Exploitation

SQLi, XSS, SSRF, CSRF, insecure deserialization — the greatest hits of the web.

💀

Binary Exploitation

Buffer overflows, ROP chains, heap exploits. We read assembly for fun.

🔬

Reverse Engineering

Ghidra, IDA, radare2. Turning binaries back into secrets.

🔑

Cryptography

RSA, AES, elliptic curves, and all the ways they can go terribly wrong.

🕵️

OSINT

Open-source intelligence gathering. You'd be surprised what's public.

🧩

Forensics

Disk images, memory dumps, steganography. CSI but with Wireshark.
